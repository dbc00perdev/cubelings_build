# assets/concept_art/

## AB'S CONCEPT ART

Scanned drawings, sketches, and brainstorms by AB. **All AB's art lives here.**

---

## FILE NAMING

Use this pattern:
```
YYYY-MM-DD_<short-description>.png
```

Examples:
- `2026-04-26_first-cubeling-sketch.png`
- `2026-05-03_purple-cottage-idea.png`
- `2026-05-10_cat-cubeling-design.png`

---

## RULES

- Every drawing AB makes for the project goes here
- Dad scans/photographs at end of session
- Anything that becomes canon gets promoted to `assets/reference/`
