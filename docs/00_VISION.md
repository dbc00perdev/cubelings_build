# 00_VISION.md

## ELEVATOR PITCH

Cubelings is a cozy 3D blocky sandbox where players build & decorate houses, care for cube-creature pets, and complete creative quests in calm, friend-only worlds. Built for kids 6-14 who love Minecraft's creativity but want zero combat, zero monsters, and a fabulous personal style.

---

## THE PROBLEM IT SOLVES

Minecraft is brilliant but:
- Combat & monsters are core, not optional
- Decoration is shallow (no real furniture system)
- Strangers in multiplayer = parental anxiety
- Aesthetic is rough, not "cute" or "modern"
- No quest/story structure for kids who want goals
- Pets are limited and forgettable

Cubelings keeps the **build & decorate** dopamine and removes everything else.

---

## TARGET PLAYER

- **Primary:** 8-12 year olds who play Minecraft Creative mode
- **Secondary:** 6-7 year olds (with simpler UI)
- **Edge:** 13-14 year olds who want a cozy alt to Roblox
- **Parent fit:** safe, no chat with strangers, no monetization

---

## CORE EXPERIENCE

> "I built a purple cottage with yellow flowers, my cat Cubeling sleeps on the bed I designed, the mailman Cubeling brought me a quest to find 5 mushrooms, and my friend visited my world and gave me a chair."

If a player can describe their game like that, we won.

---

## BRAND PROMISE

- **Cozy, not chaotic**
- **Creative, not competitive**
- **Cute, not cutesy**
- **Cube, always cube**
- **Calm, always calm**

---

## WHAT IT IS NOT

- Not a Minecraft clone
- Not a survival game
- Not a fighting game
- Not a multiplayer chat platform
- Not a free-to-play with microtransactions
- Not realistic / photorealistic
- Not 2.5D / isometric (full 3D, locked)

---

## SUCCESS METRICS (PHASE 1)

- AB plays it on her own phone for fun (not testing)
- AB shows it to a friend, friend wants to play
- AB completes 1 quest without dad helping
- AB asks "can we add ___" — meaning she's invested
- Game runs ≥ 30 FPS on Fire HD Max 11

**Not measuring:** downloads, revenue, reviews. This is a passion project.
